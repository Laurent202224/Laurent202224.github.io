I am originally from Luxembourg and currently pursuing my studies at ETH Zurich, where I have developed a strong passion for robotics. My interests lie particularly in motion planning, control, computer vision, and learning-based methods such as reinforcement learning.
Through my academic journey, I have built a solid theoretical foundation in robotic systems, including their dynamics, communication architectures, and hardware design. Alongside this, I have complemented my studies with numerous hands-on [projects](/projects/), where I applied these concepts in real-world scenarios.

These experiences have allowed me to gain practical proficiency with tools and technologies such as PX4, Gazebo, ROS/ROS2, Git, Linux, Python, C++, LaTeX, CAD design, and Docker. I have also had the opportunity to work with a variety of robotic platforms, including humanoids (Unitree G1), drones, and underwater vehicles.

Beyond engineering, I am highly active in sports. I compete in tennis at a competitive level and regularly spend time in the gym and running.
